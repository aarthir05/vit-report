FAQ

What format are the files? ▾

Standard .pptx files compatible with Microsoft PowerPoint (Windows & Mac) and Google Slides. No plugins needed.

Can I use these for commercial work? ▾

Yes! You can use them for client presentations, school, work, YouTube thumbnails, and personal projects. No attribution required.

Do I need special fonts installed? ▾

No. All templates use system fonts (Georgia, Calibri, Arial) that come pre-installed on Windows and Mac.

How many slides per template? ▾

Each template has 10 slides: Title, Agenda, Introduction, Section Divider, Content Cards, Key Takeaways, Pull Quote, Stats, Two-Column layout, and Thank You.

Will it work on Google Slides? ▾

Yes. Import the .pptx into Google Slides — shapes and colours import cleanly. Minor font substitutions may occur but won't affect the look significantly.
